Resources & Awareness

RESOURCES & AWARENESS : 1. Earthquakes * Drop, cover, and hold under sturdy furniture. * Stay away from windows and heavy objects. 2. Floods * Move to higher ground immediately. * Avoid walking or driving through floodwaters. * Store drinking water and essential supplies. 3. Hurricanes & Cyclones * Secure windows and doors. * Stay indoors and keep emergency kits ready. * Evacuate if advised by authorities. 4. Wildfires * Clear dry vegetation around your home. * Wear protective masks to avoid smoke inhalation. * Follow evacuation orders immediately. 5. Tsunamis * Move to high ground immediately after an earthquake near the coast. * Stay away from beaches and shorelines. * Listen to official alerts and warnings. 6. Landslides * Avoid hilly areas during heavy rainfall. * Look for cracks or unusual land movements. * Move to safer locations if signs of landslides appear. 7. Tornadoes * Take shelter in a basement or an interior room. * Avoid windows and cover yourself with sturdy objects. * Listen to weather alerts for warnings. 8. Droughts * Conserve water and store emergency supplies. * Grow drought-resistant crops in affected areas. * Follow government advisories on water usage. General Tip: Always keep an emergency kit ready, follow evacuation plans, and stay informed through official sources.
